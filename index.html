About Us

We are creative students

We help farmers in monitoring the chicken coop, including product design and technology development, so that they can relax a bit in taking care of their chickens. Like the serenity that overcomes the whole soul, these creative ideas bring a great positive impact to the world of animal husbandry.

We are students of Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia majoring in Computer Engineering. We made a product for chicken coop monitoring system. We want to solve the problems experienced by farmers, especially chicken farmers, to help them monitor their chicken coops so that they can take care of their chickens with the best care.
